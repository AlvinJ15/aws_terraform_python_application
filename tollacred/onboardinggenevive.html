BPO Credentialing by Tollanis

Welcome to the Onboarding Genevive page

Our mission is to enable our internal corporate team members to efficiently credential healthcare providers for our platform, ensuring seamless integration into our network.

We explore innovative approaches to enhance the onboarding experience for our team and the successful inclusion of healthcare providers.

We believe in providing our team and the organization with flexibility, transparency, and choice throughout the credentialing process.

We have carefully designed the onboarding process to facilitate the smooth addition of healthcare providers to our platform.







Emergency Contact Information

As part of our commitment to ensuring the safety and well-being of our team, we kindly request that you provide us with your emergency contact information.

This information will be kept confidential and will only be used in case of an emergency situation. Please take a moment to fill out the emergency contact form in your onboarding portal.

If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to our HR department. Thank you for your cooperation!







Mandatory documents upload

1. License or Certification

2. Verification of License or Certification

3. Education Verification (online verification required)

4.a Reference1

4.b Reference2

5. Experience (resume is sufficient)

6. Competency Evaluation (specialty) - must be done annually

7. Skills Checklist - must be done annually

8. Performance Evaluation - annually after one year of work

9. Criminal Background Check

10. Company Orientation Attestation (acknowledgment of the handbook is sufficient)

11. BLS

12. ACLS

13. Covid*

14. Flu*

15. Varicella*

16. MMR*

17. HepB*

18. TB*

19. Health Statement

20. Drug Screening
